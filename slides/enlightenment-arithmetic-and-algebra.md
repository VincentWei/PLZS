### 乘法的性质

1. 交换律：`$ \large{ a \times b = b \times a } $`
1. 结合律：`$ \large{ a \times b \times c = a \times (b times c) } $`
1. 乘法的单位元：`$ \large{ a \times 1 = a } $`

	
### 乘法分配律

`$$
\large{
    a \times (b + c) = a \times b + a \times c
\}
$$`
